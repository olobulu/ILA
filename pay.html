Payment Procedure

After ILA account is opened, you will need to pay directly to the Association account to generate your membership. You can choose from the following payment options:

1. PayPal Payment

Click the button below to make your payment via PayPal:

Pay with PayPal

2. M-Pesa Paybill Procedure

To pay via M-Pesa, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the M-Pesa menu on your mobile phone.
  2. Select "Lipa na M-Pesa" and then "Paybill."
  3. Enter the Paybill number: 123456
  4. Enter the Account number: ILA Account
  5. Enter the amount 'KSH 500' to pay.
  6. Confirm the payment details and submit.

Once the payment is completed, your membership will be generated.
